About Hawkai

Hawkai uses supervised and self-supervised machine learning to detect motor vehicle collisions in CCTV footage in real-time.


The Problem

When an accident occurs on the road, every second counts. The longer it takes for emergency services to reach the scene, the more likely it is that the accident will result in a fatality. However, emergency services can be dispatched only after the accident is reported or is detected by someone in a highway control room. Enter Hawkai.



The Hawkai Solution

Hawkai continuously monitors roadways and alerts traffic control centers in the event of a collision so that they can immediately dispatch emergency services to the accident scene. By lowering response times, Hawkai reduces the likelihood that a collision will result in fatalities and gets accident victims the help they need.
